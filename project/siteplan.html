Site Plan: Musembi Earthways Safaris

Organization Planning Document

1. Site Name

Musembi Earthways Safaris

This name combines a personal/cultural touch ("Musembi") with a focus on sustainable, grounded travel ("Earthways"). It suggests a journey that is both authentic and environmentally conscious.

2. Site Purpose

The site provides a gateway for international and local travelers to book eco-conscious safari experiences. It aims to showcase unique wildlife itineraries, promote conservation efforts, and provide a seamless booking interface for custom travel packages.

3. Scenarios

Scenario A: A solo traveler wants to know if there are eco-friendly safari packages that minimize environmental impact.

Scenario B: A family is looking for a 5-day itinerary that includes both wildlife viewing and cultural visits with local communities.

4. Color Schema

The palette is inspired by the natural landscape of the Savannah.

Primary: #2E4632 (Deep Green)
Secondary: #F5F1E9 (Sandstone)
Accent: #B3541E (Burnt Ochre)

5. Typography

Montserrat (Headings)

Used for all primary and secondary headings to provide a bold, modern, and professional look.

Open Sans (Body): Used for paragraphs and navigation. It offers high readability on mobile devices for travelers on the go.
